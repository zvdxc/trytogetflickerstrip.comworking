Lightwork Editor

The lightwork editor lets you build your own lightworks to upload to Flickerstrip. This web-based version allows you to edit works, but cannot upload them to your Flickerstrip due to browser security restrictions. To upload patterns to your Flickerstrip, download the smartphone app or desktop application.
