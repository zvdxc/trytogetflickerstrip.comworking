Flickerstrip

The first Wi-Fi LED strip that lets you control each pixel

Check out the successful Kickstarter campaign.

Flickerstrip is the LED light strip that lets you upload custom, animated patterns or Lightworks. Unlike other light strips that can only display a single color at a time, Flickerstrip can independently color each pixel.

Use the smartphone app to control your lights, browse uploaded Lightworks, or build your own from scratch using the intuitive graphical interface. Share your best patterns to let others enjoy your LED artwork.
